# Code of Conduct

Fleet Pi is committed to a respectful, harassment-free community for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful in language, feedback, and technical disagreement.
- Assume good intent and focus on helping others succeed.
- Share context, repro steps, and evidence when raising issues or reviewing
  changes.
- Keep discussions constructive, especially when work is experimental or in
  progress.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, intimidation, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Trolling, deliberate disruption, or hostile pile-ons.
- Sexualized language or imagery in project spaces.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies in repository discussions, issues, pull requests,
and any project spaces directly tied to Fleet Pi.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

- Do not escalate publicly.
- Contact the maintainers privately through GitHub.
- Include links, screenshots, or other context that will help with review.

Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or restrict participation when
needed to protect the community.
